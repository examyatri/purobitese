Setup Guide
How to Install Tiffo 📲
🌐
Open Chrome Browser
Android pe Google Chrome open karo aur tiffo.online/app visit karo.
⚡ Sirf Chrome me kaam karega — dusre browser me nahi
⬇️
Tap "Install for Android"
Page pe green "Install for Android" button dikhega — tap karo.
✅
Confirm Installation
Chrome ek popup dikhayega — "Install" ya "Add to Home Screen" — confirm karo.
💡 Downloading animation dikhega — thoda wait karo
🚀
Open from Home Screen
Install ke baad home screen pe Tiffo ka icon aayega. Tap karo aur enjoy karo! 🎉
🌐
Open Safari Browser
iPhone pe Safari open karo aur tiffo.online/app type karo.
⚠️ Sirf Safari me kaam karta hai — Chrome me nahi
⬆️
Share Button Tap Karo
Safari ke bottom bar me Share icon (↑ arrow wala box) pe tap karo.
💡 Screen ke neeche middle me hota hai
➕
"Add to Home Screen" Tap Karo
Share menu me neeche scroll karo — "Add to Home Screen" option milega.
✅
Add — Done! 🎉
Top right me "Add" tap karo. Tiffo home screen pe install ho jayega — bilkul native app ki tarah!
🎉 Ab seedha home screen se kholo — no browser needed!
